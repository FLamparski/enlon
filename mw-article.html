When building a skin you'll be working with your skin name in three forms, a lower-cased key, a camel cased identifier, and a localized skin name. For our skin the key would typically be 'myskin', the identifier 'MySkin', and the localized name 'My Skin'. That's the normal convention to follow, but you can use a different convention on your own, just remember that the key and identifier should only contain characters from the latin alphabet, you may have problems if the very start of the identifier is a number, neither may contain spaces, and the key must be entirely lower-case. The localized name should be the name you want people to see when they actually pick your skin and it may end up translated into another language.
